Welcome to Evanston

We are local property owners who have lived in the neighborhood for 40 years. We take pride in offering attractive, well maintained apartments and houses on leafy, residential streets.

Our properties are just a short walk (10 minutes or less) from Northwestern University, Downtown Evanston restaurants and theaters, Lake Michigan beaches and public transportation to the Chicago Loop. Enjoy newly remodeled kitchens and baths, central air conditioning, oak floors, free laundry, professional landscaping, convenient parking.

We are fully leased through 2020-21. Reach out to us for more information or to schedule a showing for 2021-22 leasing.
