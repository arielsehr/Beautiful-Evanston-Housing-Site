Pratt Ct.

Each apartment has three bedrooms, one bath, 1350 sq ft., central air conditioning, recently remodeled kitchen and bath, oak floors, free laundry in the basement. The 2nd floor apartment has a 3 season sunporch overlooking Fireman's Park. Front porch and lovely side yard for BBQs and sunbathing. Children's park adjacent. Four blocks to Northwestern, six blocks to Downtown Evanston, one block to Noyes St. shopping and the El. Residents only 24-hr street parking. Ask about garage or outdoor off-street parking.

Both apartments have living rooms and separate large dining rooms with built-in oak hutch and bay window.

Both apartments have newly remodeled "hotel style" bathrooms with pedestal sink or vanity, glass block window and porcelain tile.
